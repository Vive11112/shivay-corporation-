Manufacturing Facility

Modern Granulation Facility at Badami, Karnataka

Our manufacturing infrastructure is equipped with advanced production systems that support every stage of granulated fertilizer manufacturing, from granulation to dispatch-ready packing.

The facility is supported by efficient utilities, material handling equipment and experienced technical professionals to ensure reliable commercial-scale production.

Production Systems

Advanced Manufacturing Process

Granulation

Advanced granulation systems engineered to produce uniform, high-quality fertilizer granules with consistent physical properties.

Drying

Controlled drying systems maintain optimum moisture levels while preserving product quality and production efficiency.

Cooling

Efficient cooling systems preserve granule integrity and prepare products for screening and packaging.

Screening

Precision screening systems ensure uniform granule sizing while maintaining product consistency and quality.

Material Conveying

Integrated conveying systems provide smooth, continuous movement of materials throughout the production process.

Packing

Automated packing systems prepare products for safe storage, transportation and dispatch to customers.
