About Shivay Corporation

Delivering reliable contract manufacturing solutions through innovation, engineering excellence and uncompromising quality.

Who We Are

Specialists in Contract Manufacturing for Agricultural Products

Shivay Corporation is a specialized contract manufacturing company engaged in the design, development and commercial-scale production of fertilizer granules, bio-stimulant granules, soil conditioners and specialty agricultural input products.

Our manufacturing facility at Badami, Karnataka is equipped with advanced granulation, drying, cooling, screening, conveying and packing systems, enabling consistent quality at commercial scale.

What Drives Us

Innovation, Manufacturing Excellence & Sustainable Growth

With a strong focus on innovation, manufacturing excellence, cost competitiveness and sustainable agricultural solutions, Shivay Corporation has established itself as a trusted manufacturing partner for fertilizer and agri-input companies seeking reliable, scalable and commercially viable granulation solutions.

Innovation

We continuously improve our manufacturing processes, technologies and product capabilities to meet the evolving needs of modern agriculture.
